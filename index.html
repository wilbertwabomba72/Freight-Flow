Freight Flow

A warehouse sliding puzzle about priority cargo, pressure switches, roller doors, and smart route planning.

Game Overview

Freight Flow is a warehouse logistics puzzle game where every move matters. Slide trucks, shelves, boxes, and priority cargo through compact loading bays. Deliver red cargo, activate pressure switches, open roller doors, and guide the blue truck safely to the exit. Designed for quick sessions and satisfying problem solving, each level introduces tighter routes, deeper dependencies, and smarter warehouse layouts. The goal is simple, but the solution often requires planning several moves ahead.

Features

  • Warehouse-themed sliding puzzle gameplay.
  • Special red cargo delivery objectives.
  • Pressure switches that open roller doors.
  • Difficulty that grows from simple routes to multi-stage logistics challenges.
  • Clean controls for short, focused puzzle sessions.
